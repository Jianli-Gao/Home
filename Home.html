Welcome to my personal academic website!


I am currently a PhD candidate with Control and Power Research Group at Imperial College London. I am privileged to work with Dr. Balarko Chaudhuri and Prof. Alessandro Astolfi. My academic research interest lies in control theory and power systems. I am endeavored to propose analytical control solutions to the long-standing problem of transient stability analysis in large-scale power grids.


Feel free to contact me if you share similar interest.
